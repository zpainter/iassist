## Conference Registration

We are pleased to announce that **registration for the 2026 IASSIST conference is now open**!

This year's event will be held online between June 2 to June 5. The conference rate includes access to all events during the conference week. Prices for this year's conference are below.

### General Release

Rates
: Conference Registration: IASSIST Member - $50 USD
